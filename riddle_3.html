You come accross a wall of Heiroglyphics you must decipher.


Its not a bird, though feathered, and it has a mobile nest. Its quick in flight and, having flown, it always stops to rest. What am I? 🪶

I am nocturnal and fly without a sound,With feathers so soft, I barely make a bound.My eyes are wide and filled with wisdom rare,I hunt in silence, catching prey with care.What am I? 🦉

I have no voice, but I can be heard.I am often raised, but never stirred.People seek me when they have a question to ask,In classrooms, meetings, or any gathering task. What am I? ✋

Long and slithering, I glide through the grass, With a forked tongue, I detect prey as I pass. Some fear my venom, others seek my skin, Legless and sleek, a creature of sin. What am I? 🐍
